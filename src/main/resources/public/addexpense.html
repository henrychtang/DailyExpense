Problem

My maid used to have a written note book to keep track on what she spent. However, this is very unstructured and difficult to manage. And I don't have much time to look into it regularly.

Solution

This app is a simple way to keep track on the daily household expense in a more structural way. My maid will be responsible for the expense entries. And I can make use of the data to generate reports.
